Features

  1. Add Additional Numbers
  2. Quick Call
  3. Unlimited Calling
  4. Mobile App

Add Additional Numbers

Register up to 10 phone numbers to your account, so that you and your family can easily make international calls from any phone -- at home, work or on the go.

Quick Call

Register the numbers that you call most frequently so you don’t have to dial the full number to make a call. Just enter the speed dial code and the pound key to begin your call.

Unlimited Calling

For select destinations, call as much as you’d like at one flat rate, so you can call your friends and family abroad without worrying about running out of talk time.

Mobile App

International calling has never been easier. Our award-winning app lets you make one-tap dialing while enjoying our great rates and superior quality.

Benefits

Simple to Use

Make international calls without having to dial a pin -- just dial your access number from a registered phone and we’ll automatically recognize the number and you’re ready to start calling. And with our award-winning calling app, you can easily make calls with one-tap dialing.

Learn more »

Reliable

Our state-of-the-art technology routes millions of international minutes daily, so you can be sure that you’ll have superior voice quality regardless of the phone you’re calling from.

Learn more »

Affordable

Our low rates come with no monthly or additional fees, so you can save money with competitive rates and not have to worry about any surprises or hidden charges.
